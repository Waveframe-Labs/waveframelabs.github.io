Organization

The hub for the Aurora Research Initiative.

Waveframe Labs is an independent research organization focused on reproducible, audit-first science. We build and publish the Aurora Workflow Orchestration (AWO) method, ship the CRI-CORE tooling that makes it deterministic and auditable, and apply the stack to frontier problems— including Waveframe v4.0, a falsifiable cosmology program.

Everything here is open by default: method first, artifacts cited, human-in-the-loop by design. Text & media: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 · Code: Apache-2.0.
