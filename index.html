Organization

The hub for the Aurora Research Initiative.

Waveframe Labs is an independent research organization focused on reproducible, audit-first science. We publish the Aurora Workflow Orchestration (AWO) method, ship the CRI-CORE tooling that makes it deterministic, and apply the stack to frontier work—e.g., Waveframe v4.0 (cosmology) and the Societal Progress Simulator.

Method-first: AWO defines roles, artifacts, and falsifiability.
Deterministic tooling: CRI-CORE records run manifests and provenance.
Open artifacts: Text & media CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 · Code Apache-2.0.
